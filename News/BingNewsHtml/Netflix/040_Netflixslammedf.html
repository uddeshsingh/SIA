Netflix slammed for tech issues: Jake Paul-Mike Tyson card broadcast addled by buffering

Chris Coppola
Arizona Republic

Netflix subscribers across the country took to social media Friday evening complaining that the steaming service was buffering during the opening fight that was part of the highly touted Jake Paul-Mike Tyson card.

The broadcast began at 8 p.m. EST. Partway through the opening bout between Neeraj Goyat and Whindersson Nunes, which Goyat won by unanimous decision, it didn't take long for viewers to take to X (formerly Twitter) with complaints. ''#Netflix" was trending second only to #paultyson.

Netflix had not acknowledged the issue on its X account shortly after 9 p.m. EST, but the problem was continuing ahead of the second fight on the undercard.
