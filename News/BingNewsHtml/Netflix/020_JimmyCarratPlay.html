    Jimmy Carr at Playhouse Square in October | Did You Year?

    Several other comedy shows and more announced for Cleveland performance center

    Share this:

    • Click to share on Twitter (Opens in new window)
    • Click to share on Facebook (Opens in new window)
    • Share via SMS (Opens in new window)
    By John Benson
    PUBLISHED: December 2, 2024 at 11:54 AM EST

    The fast-paced comedic styling of Jimmy Carr has made him one of the U.K.’s top comedians. After releasing his most recent Netflix special, “Natural Born Killer,” last year, Carr announced a stateside tour that includes a show on Oct. 11 in Cleveland at Playhouse Square’s Connor Palace.

    Funnyman Adam Ray has parlayed his pandemic-inspired impersonation of Dr. Phil into a comedy gold. Having recently appeared with his regular standup material at Cleveland’s Hilarities 4th Street Theatre, the Los Angeles-based comedian —- dressed as the talk show host — will be back in Northeast Ohio for a “Dr. Phil Live” show on June 6 at Playhouse Square’s KeyBank State Theatre.

    Iliza Shlesinger, who filmed her 2022 Netflix special, “Hot Forever,” at Playhouse Square, has announced her new “The Get Ready Tour,” which includes a show Feb. 21 at the KeyBank State Theatre.

    Featuring Piero Barone, Ignazio Boschetto and Gianluca Ginoble, Il Volo recently released its latest album, “Ad Astra,” earlier this year. Next up for the Italian trio is a stateside tour that includes a show Nov. 22 at the KeyBank State Theatre.

    Millennials may not know just how big Simon & Garfunkel was during its heyday. Keeping the music of the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame-inducted act alive is the national touring show “The Simon & Garfunkel Story,” which features a live band playing nearly 30 songs. The sing-along show comes to Cleveland for a performance March 20 at the KeyBank State Theatre.

    After garnering national attention as Sebastian Maniscalco’s opening act, Pat McGann is starting to garner attention as one of the sharpest stand-ups in the comedy world. The Chicago comic has booked a show on Jan. 18 at Playhouse Square’s Hanna Theatre.

    ‘O Christmas Tea: A British Comedy’ playing in Lorain, Cleveland, Akron

    Cleveland's Valentine Love Jam features The Whispers, Con Funk Shun and Lenny Williams performing their hits on Feb. 15 at the KeyBank State Theatre.

    “The Daily Show” correspondent Josh Johnson was called "the funniest guy on the internet" by WIRED magazine. The Emmy Award-nominated writer and stand-up recently announced two shows (7 and 10 p.m.) on Jan. 31 at Playhouse Square’s Mimi Ohio Theatre.

    “Stardew Valley” gamers are invited to celebrate their love of the RPG when Symphony of Seasons comes through Northeast Ohio for a date on Oct. 12 at TempleLive Cleveland Masonic. A 35-piece orchestra will be performing the most memorable music from the game with a screen above the stage playing gameplay footage. Tickets are now on sale at Ticketmaster.com.

    Fast approaching: Dave Koz and Friends Christmas Tour, 7 p.m. Dec. 8, Connor Palace, $45 to $85 and Trampled By Turtles, 7:30 p.m. Dec. 12, Agora Theatre, $40.50 to $86.
