LEGO announces replica of Formula 1 champion Max Verstappen's Red Bull car

Kate Goodacre
Mon, December 2, 2024 at 3:30 PM UTC
2 min read

LEGO has announced a replica of Formula 1 world champion Max Verstappen's 2024 Red Bull Racing car.

The LEGO Technic Oracle Red Bull Racing RB20 is a 1:8 scale version of the car driven by Verstappen and his teammate Sergio Perez during the 2024 Formula 1 season – and follows on from similar LEGO Technic models of Mercedes and McLaren F1 cars.

The new LEGO Technic Red Bull will launch on March 1, 2025 – the same day as the new LEGO Technic Ferrari F1 car model – and is available to pre-order now priced at £199.99. There's a limit of three items per customer.


Oracle Red Bull Racing RB20 F1 Car

£199.99 at lego.com.uk

Related: The best F1 and motorsport LEGO sets for the racing fans in your life

This 1,639-piece set is aimed at builders aged 18 years and over and features moving suspension, a two-speed gearbox and V6 engine with moving pistons. It also has a rear wing that opens to replicate DRS on the real-life cars.

Equipped with replica soft Pirelli tyres, the car's livery also matches the real-life 2024 Red Bull. When assembled, the model is 63cm (25in) long, 23cm (9.5in) wide and 14cm (5.5in) tall.

Related: LEGO and Formula 1 star Lando Norris team up for ambitious project

LEGO and the Formula 1 organisation recently announced an extensive multi-year partnership ahead of the sport's 75th anniversary year in 2025.

All 10 current teams on the Formula 1 grid will be represented across new LEGO sets, the first of which were unveiled at the Las Vegas grand prix last month.

In addition to new Technic, Speed Champions, LEGO City and LEGO Duplo items, a new LEGO Icons set has been commissioned to celebrate Nigel Mansell's 1992 championship win and his Williams FW14B.
