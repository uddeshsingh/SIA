Giants Fan Council

As a valued member of the Fan Council, you get up to 30% off tickets to select regular season games at Oracle Park and access to early access to Spring Training at Scottsdale Stadium.

Presale Details

Tickets purchased through promotional ticket offers are not available to ticket brokers or ticket resellers. Tickets may not be resold or otherwise distributed to individuals not personally associated with the ticket purchaser. The sale, attempted sale or transfer of tickets purchased through this offer in violation of this policy is prohibited. Tickets purchased in violation of this policy will be revoked without refund.

Your Giants Fan Council offer is eligible for select 2025 regular season games, subject to availability, except the following:

  • Fri, April 4 vs. SEA
  • Sat, May 17 vs. Athletics
