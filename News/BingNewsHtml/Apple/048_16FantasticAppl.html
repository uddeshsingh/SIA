16 Fantastic Apple Pie Recipes to Make This Fall

Make a warm, comforting apple pie after you visit the orchard or farmers market this autumn.

By
Kim Wehby
Kim Wehby

Kim Wehby is a copy editor with over a decade of experience in the food and beverage space, including a senior editorial role at Dotdash Meredith Food Studios. She's edited and developed content for Food & Wine, Southern Living, and Eating Well.

Food & Wine's Editorial Guidelines
and
Alexis McCowan
Alexis McCowan

Alexis McCowan graduated from Howard University in 2021 and has since interned at Sage Publishing and the Pulitzer Center and contributed to Food & Wine.

Food & Wine's Editorial Guidelines
Updated on November 17, 2024

Nothing screams fall like apple pie the warm, sweet, flaky results of apple-picking season and in this roundup of apple pie recipes, we're giving it the love it deserves. Whether you're looking for a dessert with a classic pie crust or a new take on ice cream sandwiches, this collection has something unique and seasonal for every apple pie lover.

01 of 16

Dutch Apple Pie

A golden, walnut-studded streusel tops a filling of cinnamon-spiced apples and a simple homemade crust in this apple pie from F&W's former senior Test Kitchen editor Grace Parisi.

Get the Recipe
02 of 16

Old-Fashioned Apple Pie

In her no-frills, double-crust apple pie recipe, baker A.J. Perry creates a traditional dessert that exudes sweet-tart apples in a perfectly buttery shell. A touch of lemon enhances every gently spiced bite.

Get the Recipe
03 of 16

Apple Cheddar Rye Pie

Cheddar cheese and rye flour add sharpness and nuttiness to classic apple pie. A little savory and a little sweet, this pie is wholly delicious.

Get the Recipe
04 of 16

Apple Flamb Pie

Dried and fresh apples meet complex and aromatic spices like fennel, cinnamon, and cloves to enhance the crisp fall flavors of apple pie. Use a prepared flaky pie crust as the classic base, or shoot for the moon with our sweet-meets-salty pretzel-waffle cone crust.

Get the Recipe
05 of 16

Dried Apple Hand Pies

Dried apple slices are rehydrated with spiced cider, fresh lemon, and vanilla bean, turning into a saucy, caramelized autumnal filling for these beautifully browned hand pies.

Get the Recipe
06 of 16

Double-Crust Apple Pie

If there ever were a no-fail apple pie recipe, this would be the one. From former F&W editor Grace Parisi, this quintessential double-crust apple pie delivers juicy, sweet-tart apples all tucked inside an iconic crust.

Get the Recipe
07 of 16

Apple Pie Bars

As a handheld version of a streusel-topped apple pie, this apple pie bar recipe features a crumbly, nutty topping and a crisp, buttery crust.

Get the Recipe
08 of 16

Mountain Rose Apple Pie

Pastry chef Rebecca Masson of Houston's Fluff Bake Bar uses prized Mountain Rose apples in her dense apple pie featuring a sweet and crisp oat crumble topping.

Get the Recipe
09 of 16

Salted Caramel Apple Pie

In this stunning apple pie, homemade caramel sauce is drizzled over spice-rubbed apples; the beautiful lattice topping is brushed with egg wash and sprinkled with salt and demerara sugar for that just-caramelized finishing touch.

Get the Recipe
10 of 16

Deep-Dish All-American Cinnamon Apple Pie

Pastry legend Rose Levy Beranbaum brings us this beloved cinnamon apple pie. Fresh apple juices, warm spices, and butter cook down to make a sumptuous syrup that gets layered among circles of apple slices.

Get the Recipe
11 of 16

Deep-Dish Caramel Apple Pie

This deep-dish apple pie from former F&W Test Kitchen editor Kay Chun is loaded with textures, flavors, and aromas. Stacked cinnamon apple slices are topped first with homemade caramel and then with a brown sugar streusel.

Get the Recipe
12 of 16

Apple Pie la Mode Ice Cream Sandwiches

As an homage to her grandmothers, Top Chef winner and host Kristen Kish designed this dessert to represent both apple pie and snickerdoodles. An aromatic apple filling is swirled into vanilla ice cream, then the mixture gets sandwiched between two homemade cookies.

Get the Recipe
13 of 16

Double-Crust Apple-Apricot Pie

Author, pastry chef, and teacher Alice Medrich brings unexpected notes of honey and citrus to apple pie with her addition of dried apricots and orange zest.

Get the Recipe
14 of 16

Puff Pastry Apple Pie

With just four ingredients, this apple pie recipe employs store-bought puff pastry to make the golden, crisp crust. A filling of sugared apples creates a delicious canvas for adding your favorite spices as desired.

Get the Recipe
15 of 16

Cumin and Jaggery Glazed Apple Pie

A glaze of butter, jaggery, and cumin adds a striking finish to this apple pie created by cookbook author and chef Asha Gomez. Cream cheese makes an unexpected yet much-appreciated appearance in the flaky homemade crust.

Get the Recipe
16 of 16

Brown Bag Apple Pie

During the Great Depression in Joan Nalley Buchanan'ssmall hometown of Thomaston, Georgia, her grandmother learned to make apple pie in a brown bag. The paper grocery bag traps in all the moisture, creating the perfect conditions for tenderapplesnestled in a crisp, golden crust.

Get the Recipe
