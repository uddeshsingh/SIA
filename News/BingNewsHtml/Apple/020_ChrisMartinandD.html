Chris Martin and Daughter Apple Share a Sweet Slow Dance During Her Society Debut in Paris

Apple made her debutante debut at Le Bal des Débutantes in Paris on Nov. 30

By Emma Aerin Becker
Published on December 2, 2024 04:31PM EST

Chris Martin and Apple Martin shared a sweet father-daughter moment during her debutante debut.

On Saturday, Nov. 30, the 20-year-old daughter of the Coldplay frontman, 47, and his ex-wife Gwyneth Paltrow, 52, made her society debut at the Le Bal des Débutantes in Paris. At the event, Chris and Apple were spotted sharing a slow dance in a sweet video from Paris Match.

The duo can be seen chatting and laughing while they move in sync, though their conversation cannot be heard over the clip's music.

Paltrow was also in attendance at the celebratory event, sharing a carousel of photos and clips from her weekend on Instagram on Sunday, Dec. 1. "A special long weekend in Paris," the actress captioned the post. 

The first visual in the celebratory post was a video of the actress’s cloudy view from the airplane as she flew overseas. Next, she shared a mirror selfie of her and her daughter, who was wearing an elaborate custom Valentino gown, which Vogue reports took Valentino Creative Director Alessandro Michele’s atelier 750 hours to make.

The third slide showed a snap seemingly featuring Martin and their son Moses, 18, as they sat on a bench outside with their backs toward the camera.

The sweet photo was followed by a family picture of Paltrow, her mother Blythe Danner, and Moses in a white room with art pieces hanging behind them. 

Paltrow continued the carousel with another family snap, including her former husband, at the formal event. The Iron Man alum wore a glittery sheer black gown from Valentino’s spring 2025 collection for the occasion. The dress featured ruffle details throughout, mainly along the neckline, sleeves and skirt.

The carousel finished with images of Danner at the dinner table with model Boris Kodjoe and designer David Netto, and a photo of Paltrow and Apple embracing each other at the debutante event.
