How many times have you lost something important to you? If you’re human, like the rest of us, we’re willing to bet many, many times. That’s where a smart tracker can come in and save the day. If you have an Apple device, you need an AirTag. But if you have an Android phone, namely a Samsung model, you’ll want one of the Samsung Galaxy SmartTag 2 devices that you can get on sale today for Cyber Monday.

Amazon’s dropped this tracker to $20, saving you $10 off its usual $30 price tag. At 35% off, Samsung’s latest tracker becomes an even more useful addition to your smart home ecosystem. It also means you won’t be losing your keys anymore, hopefully. You’ve really got to stop doing that.

Your key to finding anything and everything in minutes

The Samsung Galaxy SmartTag 2 transforms everyday items into findable treasures. Its completely redesigned form factor and extended battery life make it a great option as far as smart trackers go. And it’s super useful, no matter what you need to find, like having a tireless search party in your pocket that runs for up to 500 days on a single charge.

Its IP67 water and dust resistance means it can handle life’s mishaps, while the integration with SmartThings Find feature gives you an intuitive tracking experience. The Compass View feature is particularly impressive – it doesn’t just tell you something is nearby, it guides you right to it.

This isn’t just a simple tracker – it’s also a smart home companion. Beyond finding lost items, it can trigger your compatible IoT devices, adding another layer of automation to your daily routine. The Power Saving Mode extends battery life by up to 40%, ensuring your tagged items stay findable even longer.

At $20, this represents Samsung’s vision of what item tracking should be. Think about it – you’re getting robust water resistance, extended battery life, and smart home integration in a compact device that’s both stylish and functional. The optional Silicone and Rugged cases add both protection and personality.

Next time you think you’ve lost something, don’t freak out. Remember you have a SmartTag 2, knowing you’ve got Samsung’s most advanced tracking technology keeping tabs on your valuable items, and just start your search. And if you aren’t the forgetful one in your family, pass one on to someone who is. Cyber Monday is the perfect time to do it. You may as well stock up while they’re at the lowest price ever.
