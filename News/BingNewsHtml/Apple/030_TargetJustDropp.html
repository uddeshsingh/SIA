The iPad Air is our best iPad of 2024 because it’s the ideal tablet for both casual and professional use, from watching videos, listening to music, getting creative with the Apple Pencil, to getting some work done using the Apple Smart Keyboard Folio or Apple Magic Keyboard. This is the most versatile iPad you can get, and the last day to get this deal at Target.

The 2024 iPad Air is nearly as powerful as the iPad Pro but at a much lower price point. It comes in two sizes, an 11" display with 2360-by-1640 (264ppi) resolution and a 13" display with 2732x2048 (264ppi) resolution. The display is superior to the one found on the standard iPad model with more premium features like P3 wide color, True Tone, and an ultra-low reflectivity film that helps combat glare and direct sunlight. Upgrades over the previous gen iPad Air include an M2 chip that's 15% faster in CPU performance and 25% faster in GPU performance over the M1, compatibility with the Apple Pencil Pro and Magic Keyboard, and adoption of newer connectivity standards like Wi-Fi 6E and USB Type-C.
