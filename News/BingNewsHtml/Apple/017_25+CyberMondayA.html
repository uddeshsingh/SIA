December 2024

Apple devices are expensive, and worthwhile discounts are hard to find. If you were waiting to pull the trigger on a new iPad, Apple Watch, or MacBook, today is the last day of the year you’ll find a good deal.

I’ll be honest: The Apple products we’ve rigorously tested are worth paying full price for. The company issues major software upgrades every year with new features that even older devices benefit from, which means the iPad or MacBook you buy today will remain fresh for years to come (with some care and cleaning, of course).

But every dollar counts, which is why the Wirecutter Deals team has been tracking prices on all the Apple gear we recommend to curate the Cyber Monday discounts that are worth your time.

Whether you’re looking for a gift for a loved one or a gift for yourself—I’m personally on the hunt for a new MacBook—now is the perfect time to buy.

Amazon deal price: $400; street price: $470
Best Buy deal price: $400; street price: $470

What we like: A good pair of wireless Bluetooth headphones worth considering if you’re willing to pay more for the absolute best active noise cancellation and a more-luxurious design. Sleek and distinctive look. Pairs with Apple devices effortlessly. Excellent call quality. Replaceable battery.
