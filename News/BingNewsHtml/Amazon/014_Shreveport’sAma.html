Shreveport’s Amazon fulfillment center tackles its first Cyber Monday

By Daffney Dawson and Donna Keeya
Published: Dec. 2, 2024 at 12:42 PM CST|Updated: 31 minutes ago

SHREVEPORT, La. (KSLA) - The Amazon Robotics Fulfillment Center in Shreveport marked its first Cyber Monday on Dec. 2.

Officials say Cyber Monday is the busiest online shopping day of the year, with millions of customers making purchases ahead of the holidays.

KSLA’s Donna Keeya toured the facility to see first-hand how the center handles orders during this busy time. She got a special behind-the-scenes tour of the facility.

The facility, called SHV1, is considered a “first mile” network, meaning it’s the first place for someone’s package in the flow of materials in the supply chain before it’s taken to one of Amazon’s “middle mile” centers. Then, a delivery service provider drops the packages off at people’s homes/businesses.

“Like millions of shoppers across the country, more than likely you’re online right now trying to grab some of those biggest deals. And you’ll find them right here at Amazon.com,” said Divina Mims, Amazon regional public relations manager.

“On a normal day here at SHV1, we can move and process up to about half a million packages. At the height of peak, we’re expected to nearly double that and process a million packages per day.”

At SHV1, employees pick, pack and ship items like books, toys, housewares and electronics. The facility is five floors high and covers more than 2.5 million square feet. That’s about the size of 50 football fields.

On a typical day, about a million items pass through the facility, which is the second of its kind of open in Louisiana in 2024.

Amazon officials say Cyber Monday of 2023 was the single biggest shopping day in the company’s history. Prime members bought more items that day than on any other day in the company’s history. And sales from independent sellers (mostly small- and medium-size businesses) exceeded $1 billion on Cyber Monday of 2023.

For Cyber Monday last year, Amazon says some of the best-selling categories were home, fashion, toys, beauty and Amazon devices such as the Kindle. Specifically, the Echo Dot and Fire Stick TV were among the best sellers.

The company also says a significant number of purchases were made through the Amazon app, setting a new record for mobile shopping. And Amazon says it achieved its fastest delivery times for Cyber Monday orders, with many items delivered within hours of being purchased in some areas.

At SHV1, hiring continues on a rolling basis. Starting pay for employees at the facility is $18.50 per hour. In addition, most employees get access to benefits when they start, including healthcare, dental and vision coverage; a 401k with a 50% company match; and up to 20 weeks of paid parental leave.
