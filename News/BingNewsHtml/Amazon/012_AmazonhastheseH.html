Amazon has these HotPal palm-sized rechargeable hand warmers on sale for up to 46% off

  • Updated: Dec. 02, 2024, 5:56 p.m.
  • |Published: Dec. 02, 2024, 5:55 p.m.
By
  • Danielle Halibey | NJ Advance Media for NJ.com

It’s official, the unseasonably warm fall we were having in New Jersey looks like it’s coming to an end...

And with cold temperatures and a snowy winter on the horizon, you’ll definitely want to stock up on hand warmers you can whip out whenever you’re in need. Amazon has some hot Cyber Monday deals happening on OCOOPA HotPal Rechargeable Hand Warmers and, if you shop now, you can get your secret warmup weapon for up to 46% off.

All eight of the available colorways, from Black and Pink to Orange and Ocean Wave, are marked down, starting from $15. But the deals that will save you the most are on the Green and Purple options, which you can get for $20, instead of $37.

These handy devices heat up quickly with three adjustable temperature levels that will help keep your appendages warm whether you’re walking the dog, strolling through a walk-through holiday light trail or standing on a train platform or at the bus stop and shivering as you wait for your ride to arrive.

According to the company, the long-lasting 5200mAh battery not only keeps the warmth going for up to eight hours, but can also charge your electronic devices on the go with the built in USB-C charging port.

Made from durable, aircraft-grade aluminum and ABS material, the portable palm warmers are built to last and easy to hold, thanks to their anti-skid design.

Shop the HotPal Rechargeable Hand Warmers for as little as $15 during Amazon’s huge Cyber Monday sale.
