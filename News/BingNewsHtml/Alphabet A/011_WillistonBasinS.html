Williston Basin School superintendent to resign

By Michael Anthony
Published: Dec. 2, 2024 at 5:10 PM CST|Updated: 1 hour ago

WILLISTON, N.D. (KUMV) - Williston Basin School District Superintendent Richard Faidley submitted a letter of resignation on Monday.

In the letter, Faidley said he’s proud of several accomplishments made during his time, including passing a bond for the new elementary school and opening a new career and technical education center.

This announcement comes following a discussion on Faidley’s recent evaluation and continuation of a personal improvement plan.

The board will discuss the resignation later Monday night.
