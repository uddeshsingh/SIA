Letter: Slate brings a needed fresh perspective

  • Nov 2, 2024
  • Nov 2, 2024
  • 0
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email
  • Print
  • Save

If you've not voted yet, please consider choosing one of the Grassroots Slate candidates for City Council (Ward 1- Beckett Hunt, Ward 2- Dylan de Honor, Ward 6- Alison Bowden, Ward 7- Roy Rheuben, or Ward 9- Karen Jean Canan).

These candidates, with their youth, enthusiasm and commitment, will bring a fresh perspective that is crucially needed as we all attempt to create a just, secure and sustainable future for all of our community.

Barry Reeves

Corvallis
